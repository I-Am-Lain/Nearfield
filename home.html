        Nearfield was created by 
        Brandon Fonneman, John Lovitsch and William Vetter.

        It is a 3D model of data pulled from NASA's public API, 
        available at https://api.nasa.gov/
        
        You can sort ~15,000 NEOs by their type: 
             Active 
             Inactive 
             miscellaneous Debris 

        Click to highlight a specific satellite, 
        opening a window with more data, 
        and (usually) enlarging the point in space that its represented by.

        Click again to un-highlight the satellite, 
        or click a different one to compare relative location!
